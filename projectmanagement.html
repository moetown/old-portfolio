Volvo Cars

My employment as Group Design Leader for Volvo Cars was higly communicative and design driven. I was assigned for a project leader role with design responsibility in three different exterior design car projects. These were projects proceeding in different parts of the product development phase aiming for a future global market. My specific area was exterior trim and roofing. The role was highly interactive between design, construction, suppliers, assembly, technical prerequisites and production. I worked on the project from April 2016-December 2016. I decided to have a leave from my employment to continue to study Webb development and Interaction Design.
