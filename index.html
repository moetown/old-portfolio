Hi, I am Moa Parsland. An User Experience Designer currently employed by Yovinn AB.

I'm specialised in

User testing

User testing with mock-ups, prototypes and concept ideas constantly makes me amazed with the new and value-adding information that can be unfolded.

Illustrations

Creativity is my best tool - through illustrations I can capture stories about a given situation or scenario to deliever messages with a directed emphasis on detail.

Conceptualisation

Concepting is about aquiring enough knowledge about the users to be able to estimate and predict a concepts feasibility. It is a iterative and constant introspective experience for me.

Responsive Design

With todays ever changing devices, digital media design requires ways to withold the experience throughout all possible outputs. HTML5 and CSS3 are tools I use to achieve this for webb.

Design Management

I believe that the communication and handling of the project with its surroundings is of great importance when it comes to aquiring knowledge: it's about curiousity and transparency around others.

Interaction Design

It's what's happening in the field that can teach us about the reality, weather or not we will design for it. After all, if you don't know what you might need - you won't ask for it.
