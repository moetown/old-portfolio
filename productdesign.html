Chalmers University of Technology

The project concerned how to design and build a functional piece of office furniture for the Human Factors department at Chalmers University of Technology. My responsibility was to translate the needs of the department into design drawings, calculate the amount of material and then together with a colleague build the actual pieces on spot. We built a modular stairs that could be used to sit on or to to take apart and use as a stage for presentations of the students. The project was carried out June 2015 - August 2015.
